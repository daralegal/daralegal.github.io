About

Dara is a lawyer serving the Greater Toronto Area. Prior to starting DARA LEGAL, he articled at a full-service boutique law firm in downtown Toronto. There, he gained experience handling civil and family cases and appeared regularly to argue at spousal sponsorship appeal hearings.

Dara has appeared before the Superior Court of Ontario, the Ontario Court of Justice, the Immigration and Refugee Board of Canada, and the Landlord and Tenant Board. He is fluent in English and verbally fluent in Khmer.

He is passionate about advocacy and committed to working with his clients to achieve a fair and positive outcome.
